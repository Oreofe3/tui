Fiction Books

Various books exist such as biography, autobiography, fiction, non-fiction, classics, humour books, professional books,mystery books and many more.

However, the kind of books I love to read are fiction books.

Fiction books are books that are not based on real life events. They are completely written based on the imagination of the author. I love to read fiction books because they are very interesting and explore different areas of endeavour.

  • The Secret Garden


  • Stigma
